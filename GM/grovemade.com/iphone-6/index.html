PRE-ORDER INFORMATION

WHAT IS A PRE-ORDER?

A pre-order means you are purchasing a product that is yet to be constructed. You are effectively saving a place in line while production gears up with a fully refundable payment. If you are uncomfortable with this concept, we certainly understand. Please sign up to receive updates instead below. 

WHEN ARE YOU SHIPPING?

Our iPhone 6 products are designed to fit perfectly. We don’t build on assumptions and with Apple’s keeping everything - aspect, dimension, proportion - about their newest product a closely guarded secret, we can’t start setting up for manufacturing until we have the phones in hand.

Grovemade is completely committed to finishing our new line of products for iPhone 6 and getting them onto your phone as quickly as possible. Being in the earliest stages of our production process, we’re unable to give shipping estimates at this time. There are too many unknown variables that could lead to setbacks and delays. We’re not into making promises we can’t keep and if we can’t guarantee it, we can’t get behind it. What we can guarantee is a product of unmatched quality and distinction.

As we continue to build our inventory for bumpers, cases, and wood and leather cases, shipping estimates will soon follow. We have begun to ship small batches of bumpers and cases and our goal is to ship out the first batches of all the new cases before the start of the new year.
